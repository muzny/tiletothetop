How To Play

Playing Tile To The Top is easy. Simply drag the tiles to form words that match the definitions.

You can also click on a solution tile and simply type the letter you want to be moved there. Clicking on a selected solution tile will de-select it.

Note that if the letter you typed does not have a corresponding Tile in the yellow area below, then nothing will happen.

If you need help, there are 4 hint buttons, clicking one will reveal one letter at random from the solution for that word.

To win, solve all 4 words correctly.
